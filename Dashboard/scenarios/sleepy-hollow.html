🎃 The Legend of Sleepy Hollow

Spoiler-free strategy guide — Homebrew by The Beard

Premise: The Headless Horseman rides again in Sleepy Hollow. A coven of witches fuels the specter through a dark ritual. Descend into the haunted glen and banish it forever.
Players: 1–4 | Difficulty: Medium-Hard | Length: ~2 hours
Primary challenge: Combat (Horseman) + investigation (truth) + horror resistance (haunted locations)

Key Mechanics

  • The Headless Horseman — Recurring powerful enemy. High fight, high health. You'll face him multiple times.
  • Witches' ritual — Doom pressure. The ritual makes the Horseman stronger over time. Stop it before he's unstoppable.
  • Haunted locations — Failing an investigation at a haunted location causes horror. Willpower 3+ is the safety threshold.
  • Horror-heavy encounter deck — Lots of treacheries that deal horror. Sanity and willpower are critical survival stats.
  • Investigation to discover truth — You need clues to understand how to stop the ritual and banish the Horseman.

Strategy Tips

  • Willpower 3+ is mandatory. Below that, haunted locations and horror treacheries will destroy you. Don't bring will 1–2 investigators.
  • You need a dedicated fighter. The Horseman is tough and recurring. Someone needs combat 4+ with damage-dealing weapons.
  • Investigation is how you win. Combat buys time, but clues advance the solution. Balance both.
  • Sanity soak matters. Allies with sanity (Peter Sylvestre, Beat Cop) absorb horror that would kill you.
  • Agnes is secretly amazing here. Horror fuels her ability — the scenario's horror-heavy nature becomes her power source.
  • Don't let doom accumulate. The witches' ritual advances the agenda. Deal with doom sources proactively.
⚠️ Common mistakes:
  • Bringing low-willpower investigators (Winifred, Monterey Jack) — they'll be horror-killed before contributing.
  • Ignoring the Horseman to focus on clues — he'll keep attacking and draining your health/sanity.
  • Not bringing sanity soak — even high-sanity investigators can be overwhelmed by sustained horror.

Recommended Teams (3 players)

🃏 Team 1 (ideal): Roland + Agnes + Jacqueline — Roland fights the Horseman, Agnes's horror = free damage (scenario feeds her) + Will 5 resists Haunted, Jacqueline's Will 5 + chaos control protects team on Haunted tests. All horror-resistant.
🃏 Team 2 (alternate): Lily Chen + Harvey + Jacqueline — Lily's discipline stances fight the Horseman + Mystic access for Will-based Haunted tests. Harvey (Int 5) discovers truth fast. Jacqueline protects both on Haunted tests + token control.
🌟 Team 3 (theoretical best): Zoey + Gloria + Norman — Zoey's engagement resources sustain repeated Horseman fights + Zoey's Cross pings damage on engage. Gloria neuters the horror encounter deck entirely with encounter manipulation. Norman (Int 5 + Mystic access) discovers truth + has spell backup. Build decks for: Gloria, Norman.

Recommended Teams (2 players)

🃏 Specialist: Zoey + Harvey — Zoey fights the Horseman (Combat 4 + weapons), Harvey (Int 5 + Will 4) discovers truth + passes Haunted tests.
🃏 Generalist: Pete + Agnes — Agnes (Will 5) dominates Haunted + horror-ping damages the Horseman for free. Pete flexes clues + backup combat with Duke.
⚡ Synergy: Agnes + Jacqueline — Double Will 5 makes Haunted trivial. Jacqueline prevents Agnes's Shrivelling from backfiring on the Horseman fight. Agnes turns the horror-heavy encounter deck into free damage.
