🎬 The Blob That Ate Everything

Spoiler-free strategy tips for standalone scenarios

The Blob That Ate Everything

Premise: A massive alien organism is consuming everything in its path. Stop it before it devours Arkham.
Players: 1–4 (designed for large groups) | Difficulty: Hard | Length: ~120 min
Primary challenge: Sustained high damage output against swarms + a massive boss

Key Mechanics

  • The Blob — Massive HP enemy that grows over time. Requires coordinated team damage.
  • Enemy swarms — Constant stream of smaller enemies that distract from the main threat.
  • Damage to advance — Acts advance by dealing enough total damage, not by gathering clues.
  • Scaling difficulty — More players = more enemies, but also more actions.
  • Limited locations — Tight map means enemies stack up at your location.

Strategy Tips

  • Bring weapons. Lots of weapons. Every investigator should have some combat capability.
  • Action economy matters. Free damage (Counterpunch, Guard Dog, retaliatory effects) is premium.
  • Don't over-invest in investigation. Clue requirements are minimal.
  • Burst damage beats sustained pings. Enemies have moderate-to-high HP.
  • Healing and soak matter. Long scenario drains health/sanity.
  • Coordinate targets. Focus fire on one enemy at a time.
⚠️ Common mistakes:
  • Building a pure clue deck — nothing to contribute in combat.
  • Ignoring small enemies to focus on the Blob — they'll overwhelm you.
  • Not bringing enough ammo/charges — sustained scenarios drain limited-use weapons.

Recommended Teams (3 players)

🃏 Team 1 (ideal): Zoey + Agnes + Nathaniel — Zoey primary fighter (resources from engagement), Agnes spell-fights + horror pings free damage, Nathaniel bursts massive event damage. Three damage sources that don't compete.
🃏 Team 2 (alternate): Zoey + Lily Chen + Stella — Zoey primary fighter (engagement resources). Lily's discipline stances add sustained combat from a different angle. Stella off-fights with Chainsaw + fail-forward bonus actions in the grind.
🌟 Team 3 (theoretical best): Zoey + Lily Chen + Agnes — Three distinct combat styles. Zoey generates resources from swarms. Lily's discipline stances give sustained combat from a different angle. Agnes adds willpower-based spell fighting + horror pings. Build deck for: Lily Chen.

Recommended Teams (2 players)

🃏 Specialist: Zoey + Agnes — Zoey primary fighter (engagement resources from swarms), Agnes spell-fights + horror-pings add free damage. Two sustained damage sources.
🃏 Generalist: Lily + Agnes — Lily's discipline stances + Agnes's spells = two different combat styles. Both can handle being swarmed independently.
⚡ Synergy: Nathaniel + Zoey — Zoey engages swarms (resources + Cross damage), Nathaniel retaliates with Counterpunch (free, triggers his +1 damage). Double Guardian aggro — enemies melt between engagement pings and event bursts.
