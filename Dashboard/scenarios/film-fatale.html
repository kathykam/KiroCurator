🎬 Film Fatale

Spoiler-free strategy tips for standalone scenarios

Film Fatale

Premise: Chaos erupts on three different movie sets — horror, prehistoric, and sci-fi. Quell the madness before it spills into reality.
Players: 1–4 | Difficulty: Medium | Length: ~90 min
Primary challenge: Investigation + managing multiple branching locations with moderate enemies

Key Mechanics

  • Multiple movie sets — Different locations with different themes. You may need to split up.
  • Clue-heavy advancement — Acts require clues to progress.
  • Moderate enemies — Not a combat gauntlet, but enemies need handling.
  • Narrative choices — Decisions affect which path you take.
  • Replayability — Different movie sets as "main feature" change the scenario.

Strategy Tips

  • Prioritize clue efficiency. Multi-clue cards (Deduction, Pilfer, Rite of Seeking) are premium.
  • Bring some combat. One dedicated fighter keeps enemies from stalling your clue-getters.
  • Movement matters. Multiple sets = lots of locations. Free movement saves tempo.
  • Balance your team. Flex investigators (Agnes, Jenny, Winifred) shine here.
  • Don't split up too early. Concentrate efforts, clear one area, then move on.
⚠️ Common mistakes:
  • Over-specializing in combat — you'll fall behind on clues.
  • Spreading across all locations at once — concentrate, then expand.
  • Ignoring enemies entirely — they'll stack up and overwhelm you.

Recommended Teams (3 players)

🃏 Team 1 (ready to play): Rex + Jenny + Roland — Rex vacuums clues (★★★ here), Jenny flexes with resources + movement between sets, Roland handles enemies + Crowbar clues. All ready to play.
Team 2: Rex + Rogue (Monterey OR Jenny) + Roland — Rex vacuums clues, Rogue adds movement/flex (Monterey is faster, Jenny is more flexible), Roland handles enemies + contributes clues on kills.
Team 3: Daisy + Winifred + Mystic (Agnes OR Jacqueline) — Int clues (Daisy) + agility clues (Winifred's Pilfer) = two clue sources from different stats. Mystic flexes between investigation spells and combat.
