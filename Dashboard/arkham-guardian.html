Zoey Samaras — The Chef

🧠 Will 4 📖 Int 2 👊 Combat 4 🦶 Agility 2 ❤️ 9 HP 🧠 6 Sanity
Ability: After you become engaged with an enemy → gain 1 resource.
Elder Sign: +1. If attack succeeds, deal +1 damage.
Sig: Zoey's Cross (deal 1 damage on engage). Weakness: Smite the Wicked (enemy spawns far, trauma if alive at end).

Tips for Playing Zoey

  • Embrace engagement. Every enemy = free resource + Zoey's Cross damage.
  • Taunt is your money printer. Fast, engage ALL enemies at location. Multiple triggers per play.
  • "Get behind me!" protects teammates. Redirect attacks to you — Zoey wants to be hit (9 HP, Guard Dog retaliates).
  • Brand of Cthugha uses willpower (4) to fight. Bypasses combat stat for spell-based attacks.
  • Low intellect (2) means don't investigate. Let Rex/Jenny handle clues. Your job is killing.

Roland Banks — The Fed

🧠 Will 3 📖 Int 3 👊 Combat 4 🦶 Agility 2 ❤️ 9 HP 🧠 5 Sanity
Ability: After you defeat an enemy → discover 1 clue at your location.
Elder Sign: +1 for each clue on your location.
Sig: Roland's .38 Special (+1 combat per clue at location). Weakness: Cover Up (2 clues to clear, trauma if not).

Tips for Playing Roland

  • Kill hostile guests, don't avoid them. Each kill = free clue from his ability.
  • Crowbar investigates with combat 4. Roland becomes a flex investigator — fights AND gets clues.
  • Physical Training boosts Parley. Spend 1 resource → willpower 4 for Parley tests.
  • Deduction (Lv 2) + Crowbar = massive clue burst. Succeed by 2 → discover 3 clues in one action.
  • Pathfinder gives free movement. Reach hostile guests or clue locations without wasting actions.
  • Cover Up: Clear it early. Roland's ability + Crowbar discover clues constantly — put them on Cover Up first.
  • Low sanity (5) is your weakness. Guts helps, but avoid horror-heavy situations.
