🔰 Roland Banks — The Fed

Guardian flex investigator — fights AND gets clues

Roland Banks

🧠 Will 3 📖 Int 3 👊 Combat 4 🦶 Agility 2 ❤️ 9 HP 🧠 5 Sanity
Ability: After you defeat an enemy → discover 1 clue at your location.
Elder Sign: +1 for each clue on your location.
Deckbuilding: Guardian 0–5, Seeker 0–5, Neutral 0–5.
Roland's .38 Special (Hand, Firearm) — +1 combat, +1 damage. Gets +1 combat for each clue on your location. Scales with investigation progress.
Cover Up — Starts with 2 clues on it. If Cover Up still has clues when the game ends, suffer 1 mental trauma. Discover clues at your location to remove them from Cover Up instead.

Why Roland for Excelsior

Roland is secretly one of the best investigators for Excelsior Hotel:
  • Clue on kill — When guests go hostile via Driven to Madness, killing them gives you a free clue. You're solving the problem AND advancing.
  • Crowbar — Investigate using combat 4 instead of intellect 3. Roland becomes a 4-stat investigator.
  • Fine Clothes — Boosts Parley to int 4 or will 4. Roland can actually Parley guests reliably.
  • Seeker access — "I've got a plan!" (Lv 2), Deduction, Pathfinder — tools other Guardians can't take.

Tips for Playing Roland

  • Kill hostile guests, don't avoid them. Unlike other investigators who evade, Roland WANTS to kill — each kill = free clue from his ability.
  • Crowbar is your primary investigation tool. Combat 4 + Crowbar = investigate at 4 effective. Better than his base intellect 3.
  • Cover Up is manageable. You're already discovering clues constantly (ability + Crowbar + Evidence!). Just make sure to be at a location with clues when you discover them.
  • Low sanity (5) is your weakness. Horror treacheries are dangerous. Guts helps, but be careful in horror-heavy rounds.
  • "I've got a plan!" (Lv 2) is burst damage. Deals damage equal to clues on your location (up to 3). Save it for tough enemies.
  • Pathfinder saves actions. Free move to reach hostile guests or clue-rich locations without spending an action.

Mitigating Cover Up

Cover Up has 2 clues. When you discover clues at your location, you can place them on Cover Up instead of on your investigator card.

  • Clear it early — don't let it linger until the end.
  • Roland's ability (clue on kill) can place that clue on Cover Up.
  • Crowbar investigations also work — discover a clue, put it on Cover Up.
  • Only 2 clues needed — usually cleared by round 2-3 if you prioritize it.

🃏 Roland Banks — Excelsior Hotel (9 XP standalone)

Cards from: Core Set ×3 + Dunwich + Edge of the Earth + Drowned City. No overlap with Zoey's deck.

  • 2× Machete
  • 2× Crowbar (Drowned City)
  • 2× Guard Dog
  • 2× Fine Clothes
  • 2× Flashlight
  • 2× Dodge
  • 2× Emergency Cache
  • 2× Ready for Anything (Drowned City)
  • 2× Vicious Blow
  • 2× Perception
  • 2× Guts
  • 2× Unexpected Courage
  • 1× Beat Cop (Lv 2)
  • 1× Pathfinder (Lv 1)
  • 2× "I've got a plan!" (Lv 2)
  • 1× Deduction (Lv 2)

Deck Strategy Notes

  • Mulligan for: Machete or Crowbar. Machete for combat, Crowbar for investigation. Either one gets you rolling.
  • Crowbar + Machete = full flex. Investigate with Crowbar (combat 4), fight with Machete (+1 combat, +1 damage). Roland does both at 4+ effective.
  • "I've got a plan!" (Lv 2) deals up to 3 damage using intellect. Save for tough enemies or the Horseman-equivalent in Excelsior.
  • Beat Cop (Lv 2) — exhaust for 1 damage. Repeatable ping to finish off wounded enemies.
  • Pathfinder — free move once per round. Reach hostile guests without wasting an action.
  • Deduction (Lv 2) — commit to a Crowbar investigation for +1 intellect AND discover 2 extra clues on success by 2+. Massive clue burst.
  • Ready for Anything — draw 1 card (2 if you drew an enemy this round). In Excelsior, enemies appear often = consistent 2-card draw.
  • Fine Clothes — +1 to Parley. Roland at will 3 + Fine Clothes = 4, or int 3 + Fine Clothes = 4. Passes the difficulty 3 Parley test reliably.

XP Breakdown (9 XP)

  • 2× "I've got a plan!" (Lv 2) = 4 XP — burst damage using intellect
  • 1× Beat Cop (Lv 2) = 2 XP — repeatable ping damage
  • 1× Deduction (Lv 2) = 2 XP — extra clues on Crowbar success
  • 1× Pathfinder (Lv 1) = 1 XP — free movement
