🔬 Chapter 2 Core Set — Buy Research

Hypothetical: If I buy Chapter 2, how do my scenario teams improve?

The 5 New Investigators

Daniela Reyes — Guardian

Will 3 / Int 2 / Com 5 / Agi 2 / 9 HP / 5 San

Ability: After an enemy attacks an investigator at your location → Fight that enemy for free. (Once per round.)

VERDICT: MAYBE. Combat 5 + free retaliatory fight = powerful. BUT her ability triggers on Attacks of Opportunity timing, requiring coordination with teammates and rules knowledge. Community rates her Simplicity ★★☆☆☆. Conflicts with your CPU Rule — per-turn positioning decisions and timing disputes. Zoey is simpler and already built.

Replaces: Zoey in Blob teams IF you don't mind the complexity. Otherwise Zoey stays.

Joe Diamond — Seeker

Will 2 / Int 4 / Com 4 / Agi 2 / 7 HP / 7 San

Ability: After you successfully investigate → Draw 1 card. (Once per round.)

VERDICT: BUY. Int 4 + Combat 4 = true flex investigator. Investigates AND fights without needing special cards. Card draw on investigate keeps hand full. Better than Roland for Film Fatale (higher intellect, same combat).

Replaces: Roland in investigation-heavy scenarios. Joe does what Roland does but with better intellect and built-in card draw.

Trish Scarborough — Rogue

Will 2 / Int 4 / Com 2 / Agi 4 / 8 HP / 6 San

Ability: Take an additional action each turn that can only be used to evade.

VERDICT: MAYBE. Free evade action is great for Excelsior (handle enemies without killing). Int 4 + Agi 4 = clues + evasion. But Jenny already fills the Rogue flex slot and has better economy.

Replaces: Jenny in Excelsior (free evade = never kill innocents). Doesn't replace Jenny everywhere — Jenny's resource generation is unique.

Dexter Drake — Mystic

Will 5 / Int 2 / Com 2 / Agi 3 / 6 HP / 8 San

Ability: After you play an asset → return another asset to hand OR play a different asset from hand. (Once per round.)

VERDICT: SKIP. Will 5 is great but Agnes already covers Mystic with Will 5 + damage pings. Dexter's asset cycling is powerful but complex — adds fiddliness you don't enjoy. Agnes is simpler and already built.

Replaces: Nobody. Agnes is better for your playstyle (straightforward spell combat vs Dexter's asset juggling).

Isabelle Barnes — Survivor

Will 4 / Int 2 / Com 3 / Agi 3 / 5 HP / 9 San

Ability: Take 1 direct horror → commit a skill from your discard pile to a test. (Once per round.)

VERDICT: MAYBE. Recurs skills from discard = never runs out of commits. 9 sanity fuels her ability for 8+ uses. But Pete already covers Survivor with Duke (better stats via ally). Isabelle is a different style — commit-heavy vs Pete's asset-heavy.

Replaces: Could replace Pete in teams where you want more consistent skill tests over Duke's flexibility. Niche.

Chapter 2 Investigator Starter Decks (sold separately)

5 additional pre-built decks, like the Chapter 1 starters (Harvey, Winifred, etc.). Pick-up-and-go.

Tommy Muldoon — Guardian

Will 3 / Int 3 / Com 4 / Agi 2 / 9 HP / 6 San

Ability: Spend 1 resource or discard a Firearm → play a Firearm from hand (paying cost). Enters play ready. Cycles weapons constantly.

VERDICT: BUY. Combat 4 + 9 HP + Firearm cycling = sustained fighter who never runs out of guns. Different from Daniela (retaliatory) and Zoey (resource-based). Good for Blob and Wendigo.

Carolyn Fern — Seeker

Will 3 / Int 5 / Com 1 / Agi 3 / 6 HP / 9 San

Ability: After you heal horror from an investigator or Ally → discover 1 clue at your location. (Once per round.)

VERDICT: BUY. Int 5 = best cluever stat. Heals team horror AND gets free clues doing it. Perfect for horror-heavy scenarios (Excelsior, Sleepy Hollow). Support + clues in one package.

André Patel — Rogue

Will 2 / Int 3 / Com 2 / Agi 5 / 7 HP / 8 San

Ability: After you succeed by 2+ during your turn → take an additional action. (Once per round.)

VERDICT: MAYBE. Agi 5 + bonus action on over-success = 4+ actions/turn. Great for evasion-heavy scenarios. But Will 2 / Com 2 means he can't fight or resist treacheries. Very specialized.

Marie Lambeau — Mystic

Will 4 / Int 4 / Com 1 / Agi 3 / 8 HP / 7 San

Ability: After damage is placed on Marie → draw 2 cards, discard 1. (Once per phase.)

VERDICT: MAYBE. Will 4 / Int 4 is flexible. Card draw on damage is interesting but requires taking hits. Agnes already covers Mystic combat. Marie is more of a flex/support Mystic.

Miguel de la Cruz — Survivor

Will 3 / Int 2 / Com 3 / Agi 4 / 8 HP / 8 San

Ability: Take an additional action each turn that can only be used to play an event.

VERDICT: MAYBE. Free event action = play Lucky!, "Look what I found!", or combat events without spending a regular action. 8/8 HP/San is tanky. But Pete already covers Survivor with Duke's flexibility.

Final Bottom Line

Buy the Chapter 2 Core Set — primarily for the neutral skill reprints (Guts, Perception, Overpower, Manual Dexterity, Unexpected Courage, Emergency Cache). These solve your card-sharing bottleneck permanently and let you build more decks without conflicts.

Build priority:
1. Joe Diamond — simple, effective flex (Int 4 + Com 4 + card draw). Replaces Roland in investigation-heavy scenarios.
2. Carolyn Fern (starter) — Int 5 + healing → free clues. Unique support role nobody else fills.
3. Daniela — only if you're OK with AoO timing complexity. Skip if fiddliness bothers you; Zoey is fine.
4. Skip Trish (Jenny covers Rogue), Dexter (Agnes covers Mystic), Isabelle/Miguel (Pete covers Survivor).

Minimum spend for maximum impact: Core Set ($50) + Carolyn Starter ($20) = $70 for Joe + Carolyn + neutral reprints. Covers 4+ scenario Team 1s and solves deckbuilding constraints.
