💵 Rogue Investigators

Resourceful tricksters who bend the rules and stay one step ahead

Jenny Barnes — The Dilettante

🧠 Will 3 📖 Int 3 👊 Combat 3 🦶 Agility 3 ❤️ 8 HP 🧠 7 Sanity
Ability: Collect 1 additional resource during each upkeep phase (2 total instead of 1).
Elder Sign: +1 for each resource you have.
Deckbuilding: Rogue 0–5, Neutral 0–5, up to 5 other level 0 cards from any class.
Jenny's Twin .45s (Asset, 2 hand slots, uses X ammo) — Spend 1 ammo: Fight. +2 combat, +1 damage. Loaded with ammo equal to your resources when played. A powerhouse weapon that scales with your wealth.
Searching for Izzie — Attaches to the farthest location. Costs 2 actions to investigate and discard. If still in play at game end, take 1 mental trauma.

Tips for Playing Jenny

  • 🎯 Mulligan for: Leo De Luca or Dr. Milan Christopher. Leo gives a 4th action every turn (Jenny can afford his 6-cost). Milan funds everything else. Jenny's balanced stats mean she doesn't need a specific tool — she needs economy to boost whatever comes up.
  • Jenny is the ultimate generalist. 3/3/3/3 stats mean she's decent at everything but great at nothing. Her power comes from resources, not raw stats.
  • Resources are your superpower. With 2 per upkeep + Emergency Cache + other economy cards, you'll be swimming in money. Use it to fuel expensive assets and skill boosts.
  • Jenny's Twin .45s scale with your wealth. Play them when you have 5+ resources for a weapon with 5+ ammo, +2 combat, +1 damage. That's a lot of firepower.
  • Elder Sign gets better the richer you are. With 8 resources, your Elder Sign is +8. This makes Jenny surprisingly reliable on critical tests late-game.
  • Leo De Luca is Jenny's best friend. An extra action every turn is incredible, and Jenny can afford his 6-cost easily. Prioritize getting him out early.
  • Her 5 off-class slots let you cover weaknesses. Consider: Magnifying Glass (Seeker, investigation), Flashlight (Neutral), Peter Sylvestre (Survivor, agility + sanity), or Ward of Protection (Mystic, treachery cancel).
  • Hot Streak and Emergency Cache fuel both her Twin .45s and expensive Rogue cards. Don't be afraid to spend actions gaining resources early — it pays off.
  • Think on Your Feet escapes spawning enemies — when an enemy spawns at your location, move to a connecting location for free. Jenny stays mobile without wasting actions.

Mitigating Searching for Izzie

Izzie spawns at the farthest location and costs 2 actions + a successful investigation to clear. Mental trauma if you ignore it.

  • Don't panic. You have time — it only matters at game end. Plan your route to pass through that location naturally.
  • 2 actions is expensive. Try to clear it on a turn where you're already at or near that location for other reasons.
  • The investigation test uses the location's shroud. If it's a high-shroud location, bring Flashlight or commit skill cards.
  • In multiplayer, coordinate. If a teammate is near Izzie's location, they can't help directly (it's your weakness), but you can plan to be there together for support.
  • Worst case: If the scenario is about to end and Izzie is across the map, weigh whether 1 mental trauma is worth the actions to get there. Sometimes it's not.

Deck Strategy Notes

  • Mulligan for: Leo De Luca or Dr. Milan Christopher. Leo gives you 4 actions per turn; Milan gives +1 intellect + economy. Both are game-changing early.
  • Fine Clothes is key for Excelsior Hotel. It gives you +1 to Parley tests, making those Hotel Guest interactions much more reliable.
  • Ethereal Slip handles enemies without fighting. Move an engaged enemy to a connecting location — perfect for Jenny who'd rather not brawl.
  • Scout Ahead lets you discover clues at connecting locations without moving there. Great for efficiency when paired with Jenny's extra actions from Leo.
  • .41 Derringer is your backup weapon. With Hard Knocks to boost combat, Jenny can handle small enemies. Save it for emergencies.
  • Dr. Milan + Magnifying Glass (off-class Seeker splash) give Jenny investigation power. With 3 base intellect + 1 Milan + 1 Glass = 5 intellect, she investigates well.
  • Quick Thinking gives bonus actions when you succeed by 2+. Combined with Leo De Luca, Jenny can take 5+ actions in a turn.
  • Gené Beauregard provides flexible skill boosts and movement — perfect for Jenny's jack-of-all-trades approach.
  • Liquid Courage heals horror from teammates (or yourself). Useful in horror-heavy scenarios like Excelsior.
