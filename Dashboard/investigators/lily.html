🔮 Lily Chen — The Martial Artist

Mystic fighter with Guardian upgrades — Disciplines make her unstoppable

Lily Chen — The Martial Artist

🧠 Will 3 (+1) 📖 Int 2 (+1) 👊 Combat 4 (+1) 🦶 Agility 3 (+1) ❤️ 7 HP 🧠 7 Sanity

(+1) from Disciplines — effective stats: Will 4 / Int 3 / Combat 5 / Agility 4

Ability: You begin the game with each Discipline in play (Unbroken side). Each gives +1 to a stat + a powerful action.

Elder Sign: +2. Flip a Broken Discipline back to Unbroken.

Deckbuilding: Mystic 0, Guardian 1–5, up to 5 Guardian level 0 cards, Neutral 0–5. No Firearms.

4× Disciplines (Permanent, start in play) — +1 Will (heal 3 horror), +1 Int (draw cards), +1 Combat (+5 skill value for one test!), +1 Agility (take 3 different actions). Each flips to Broken after use.
Burden of Destiny — Revelation: Flip a Discipline to Broken, OR take 2 direct damage. Manageable — you choose which Discipline to sacrifice.

Tips for Playing Lily

  • 🎯 Mulligan for: Dragon Pole or Ritual Candles. Dragon Pole is Lily's primary weapon (+2 combat, uses charges from discipline flips). Ritual Candles boost all spell/discipline tests by +1. Her Disciplines start in play automatically — she just needs her combat tools.
  • Combat 5 baseline. With Discipline, Lily fights at 5 before any boosts. Dragon Pole or Butterfly Swords push her to 7+.
  • Combat Discipline's +5 action is your boss killer. Once per game (until Elder Sign flips it back), test at Combat 10. Save it for the big enemy.
  • Dragon Pole scales with arcane slots filled. With Ritual Candles in arcane slots, Dragon Pole gets +1/+2 combat AND +1 damage. Lily's best weapon.
  • Sweeping Kick Lv 1 uses agility + combat combined. Lily at Agility 4 + Combat 5 = testing at 9. Deals +1 damage AND auto-evades on success.
  • Toe to Toe is free guaranteed damage. 0 cost, auto-success, +1 damage. The enemy hits you back — but Lily has 7 HP to absorb it.
  • Butterfly Swords Lv 2 attacks twice. First attack at combat +1, second uses agility. Two attacks for one action = massive damage output.
  • Disciplines flip to Broken when used. Don't use them frivolously. The +1 stat is always active; the action is one-shot until Elder Sign restores it.
  • No Firearms means no .45 Auto, no Machete. Dragon Pole, Butterfly Swords, Sledgehammer, Knife are your weapons.

Mitigating Burden of Destiny

  • Choose wisely. Flip the Discipline you've already used (already Broken = no loss) or the one you need least right now.
  • 2 direct damage is sometimes better. Lily has 7 HP — if all Disciplines are critical, take the hit.
  • Elder Sign flips one back. On any test, pulling the Elder Sign restores a Broken Discipline. Keep testing.

Deck Strategy Notes

  • Mulligan for: Dragon Pole or Butterfly Swords. Lily needs a weapon in hand — her base combat 5 is great but she needs +damage.
  • Dragon Pole + 2× Ritual Candles: +2 combat from filled arcane slots, +1 damage. Lily fights at Combat 7, dealing 2 damage. Ritual Candles also boost Rite of Seeking.
  • Butterfly Swords Lv 2 attacks twice per action. First at Combat 6, second using Agility 4. Two hits = 2-3 damage per action.
  • Sweeping Kick Lv 1: Combat + Agility combined (9 total!) + 1 damage + auto-evade on success. Kill small enemies AND disengage.
  • Toe to Toe: Free, auto-success, +1 damage. Enemy attacks you back — Lily has 7 HP to absorb. Use on 2-health enemies for instant kills.
  • Bruiser Lv 3: 2 resources/round for weapons and combat boosts. Funds Dragon Pole (cost 3) and Butterfly Swords (cost 3).
  • Rite of Seeking: Investigate with Will 4. Lily can flex into clues when enemies aren't around.
  • Meditative Trance: Heal 2 horror. Lily has 7 sanity but Disciplines flipping can feel bad — Trance keeps her stable.
  • Combat Discipline (+5 action): Save for the boss. Combat 5 + Dragon Pole (+2) + Discipline (+5) = testing at 12. Nothing survives.
